About Us

The Robotics Club welcomes makers of all levels. We run weekly hands-on workshops on embedded systems, computer vision, ROS, and mechanical design. New to robotics? Perfect—our mentors will help you get from zero to building your first bot in weeks.

FAQ

Do I need prior experience?

No. We love beginners—most workshops include starter kits and step-by-step guides.

Are tools provided?

Yes. Soldering stations, microcontrollers, and common sensors are available in the lab.
